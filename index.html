Reseraches

Why do people solicit social feedback on subreddits like r/RateMe, r/RoastMe,r/ToastMe, and r/AmIUgly?

• fall 2025

Why do people decide to expose themselves in social media and what really motivates them to come to the internet for what can be best describe as social support? In this pape we delved deeper in such questions and tried to find motivations behind such social seeking behaviours.
